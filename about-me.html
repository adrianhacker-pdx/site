About Me

Semiconductors, Hardware, and Software

Originally from California, I now live in the Pacific Northwest in beautiful Portland, Oregon. I attended University at Clark College, American Public University System, and Portland State University. My majors were Electronics Technology, Computer Science, and Information Technology. I had a long tenure in the semiconductor industry working at companies like Intel, H.P., and LSI Logic now part of Renesas Semiconductor. I have held positions as a Manufacturing Technician, Maintenance Technician, Manufacturing Supervisor, and Project Coordinator in research and development.
